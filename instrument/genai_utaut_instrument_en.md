# Generative AI Adoption in Engineering Education: Reusable Instrument Structure (English)

This document provides a reusable survey structure based on the UTAUT framework, adapted for the study of generative AI adoption in engineering education.

## Recommended response scale

For perceptual items:

1 = Strongly disagree  
2 = Disagree  
3 = Neutral  
4 = Agree  
5 = Strongly agree

For usage-frequency items:

1 = Never  
2 = Rarely  
3 = Sometimes  
4 = Often  
5 = Always

---

## Performance Expectancy (PE)

- I believe that using generative AI will improve my academic performance.
- I believe that generative AI will help me better understand the concepts in my courses.
- I believe that incorporating generative AI will increase efficiency in completing my assignments and projects.
- I believe that using generative AI will make it easier for me to prepare for exams and assessments.

## Effort Expectancy (EE)

- I believe that learning to use generative AI is easy for me.
- I believe that interacting with generative AI is clear and easy to understand.
- I believe that integrating generative AI into my academic activities requires minimal effort.
- I am confident that I will be able to master the use of generative AI without difficulty.

## Social Influence (SI)

- I believe that my classmates support the use of generative AI in learning.
- I believe that my instructors actively encourage the use of generative AI.
- I believe that the educational community views the implementation of generative AI positively.
- I believe that the people who are important to me think that I should use generative AI in my studies.

## Facilitating Conditions (FC)

- I believe that my educational institution provides the technological resources needed to use generative AI.
- I believe that I have access to training on the use of generative AI.
- I believe that technical support is available to solve issues related to generative AI.
- I believe that my institution’s policies and guidelines support the integration of generative AI into learning.

## Behavioral Intention / Use Frequency Proxy

How often do you use generative AI for the following activities?

- Information search and research
- Summarising and assignment preparation
- Project and coursework development
- Exam and evaluation preparation

## Suggested demographic/contextual items

- Age
- Gender
- Current semester
- Prior experience with AI technologies

## Notes for adaptation

- Preserve the meaning of each construct when translating or adapting.
- Review terminology for local relevance.
- Align administration with local ethical procedures and institutional policy.
- Consider adding one attention-check item.
