# Instrument Materials

This folder contains reusable materials related to the survey instrument used to study generative AI adoption in engineering education.

## Contents

- `genai_utaut_instrument_en.md`  
  English version of the reusable instrument structure.

- `genai_utaut_instrument_es.md`  
  Spanish version of the reusable instrument structure.

- `construct_mapping.csv`  
  Mapping of survey items to constructs and intended interpretation.

- `administration_guidance.md`  
  Practical recommendations for survey deployment, adaptation, and contextualisation.

## Important note

These materials are shared to support replication and local adaptation.

Users should:
- review wording carefully,
- adapt terminology to their context,
- align administration procedures with local ethics requirements,
- and preserve conceptual meaning when translating or modifying items.
